About

Hi, I’m Navneet. I build websites front end, logos, icons and digital products. Design to me is a way of life. It’s not a job but a passion that you grow with every day. I’ve been a self-taught designer and currently I am enrolled in Web and Mobile App Designing at Langara College, Vancouver

I start the design with a pencil and paper,thinking about the problem and the solution. I’ll then sit down at my computer and start with wire frames and low level mockups. Then add the imagery, typography design and details. I use Photoshop and Illustrator for my layout and design.
